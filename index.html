Machine learning methods have been developed to detect Parkinson's Disease. By uploading a short clip of you saying the /a/ syllable (as in the words: after /’ɑːftə/, last /lɑːst/, and ask /ɑːsk/)

Early Signs

Loss of smell, small tremors on your hand, trouble moving? If you have early signs get checked out!
